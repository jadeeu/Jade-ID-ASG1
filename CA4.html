HOW IT ALL BEGAN

Chase Atlantic is an Australian alternative R&B band formed in 2014 by Christian Anthony and brothers Mitchel and Clinton Cave. Starting as a 2011 university project, they broke out with the EP *Dalliance* and the viral single **“Friends.”** Known for their dark alternative pop sound, they’ve since released multiple projects and gained international fame.
